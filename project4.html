Network Go-Back-N(GBN) Protocol

May. 2020 - Aug. 2020

- Implemented GBN sliding window protocol by socket programming to reliably transfer large text files from one host to another across unreliable network, such as network errors, packet loss, delay and duplication.

- Achieved 100% reliable data transfer through applying time-out and cumulative acknowledge strategy to unreliable UDP protocol. Eliminated sending and receiving conflict in one host and increased timing accuracy by multithreading.

- Proved the correctness according to the analysis of sender and receiver log files. Reported the transmission efficiency under variable conditions of different maximum packet delay and packet loss possibilities.
